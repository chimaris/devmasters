Millet-Swallow

Millet is so important because of its uniquely high content of nutrients, including impressive starch levels, very high B-vitamin content, as well as calcium, iron, potassium, zinc, magnesium, not to mention being a healthy source of essential fats in the body.

Nutritional Benefits

  • Carbohydrates: Provides energy and contains complex carbohydrates.
  • Fiber: Supports digestive health and promotes satiety.
  • Protein: A good source of plant-based protein for tissue repair and muscle growth.
  • Magnesium: Helps maintain nerve and muscle function, supports the immune system, and promotes strong bones.

Boiled-Plantain

Plantains are a carb-rich food and a good source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They also contain antioxidants that fight free radicals.

Health Benefits

  • Diabetes Control: PPlantains have a GI in the 40s, making them a good food choice for people with diabetes.
  • Digestive Health: Plantains have a fair amount of fiber, which improves bowel function
  • Blood Pressure Control: Plantains have more potassium than their yellower cousins which intake helps to reduce many cardiovascular problems
